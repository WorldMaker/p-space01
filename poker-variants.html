Variants

There are many variants of poker. Too many to list here, as there are whole web sites dedicated to just the variants of Poker. What I wanted to mention was one interesting variation I have found, using a five-suit deck (65-67 cards), such as the one you can buy from Star Deck. (An interesting trivia note: At one time five-suit decks were very popular in the United States). This of course changes the probabilities quite a bit, and adds a few new types of hands. I think this may be interesting to do a table of that...
